## 活着与坚持 
![winner and quitt er](http://loddymicucci.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/winners-never-quit..jpg)  

在梦想的激情渐渐消退后，我想找回激情，写下一篇闲谈文字。  

### What are you/we live for?  
在[yahoo问答](http://answers.yahoo.com/question/index?qid=20100922023937AA3IYMp)上有一个人这么问到：  
> I have no idea what I'm living for. I'm only 18 but I feel like I haven't accomplished anything in my 18 years and I never worked hard for anything in life. I had a lot of "wants" but I was never so passionate to work hard to attain these wants. I am starting college next week and a lot of students are all excited but I don't even care. I know that college has a lot of opportunities but now I'm thinking what am I even working for?  

这个在问为什么活着，为什么活的这么没有激情？  
在[quora](http://www.quora.com/Life/How-can-you-live-an-interesting-life)上有人问怎么活得有激情:  
>Life: How can you live an interesting life?What are some of the ways you have been successful in making your life more interesting and meaningful?    

the first answer的是这样的：  
>Three tips that I've found helpful for leading a more interesting life:  
>**Be willing to do things by yourself.** A lot of people want to take art classes or try new restaurants or travel to exotic countries, but few people consider doing these things without companions. If you're not willing to do stuff by yourself, you end up missing out on a lot of interesting opportunities because there's no one around to join you.  
>**Say "yes" to as many opportunities as possible**. If someone asks you if you'd like to go to a new restaurant or volunteer for a charity or do a weekend road trip together, say "yes". The more things you try, the more interesting your life will be.  
>**Stop caring about what is normal or expected**. Sometimes the only thing stopping you from doing something cool is that you're afraid of looking stupid or weird. The problem is that many things seemed stupid or weird until they didn't: traveling for a year after college, dropping out of school to start a tech company, joining an online dating site, etc. It's hard to veer off the beaten path and have an exceptional life if you're always looking of social acceptance.

翻译过来就是：  
承受孤独；勇于尝试；消除顾虑。  
![donn't be stupid](http://qph.cf.quoracdn.net/main-qimg-4e1bd23d43616678d20b4d673efb4383)  

### BRING PASSION BACK  
The life is so beautiful.look at the sky,look up at the evening stars.  

我开始在网上寻找各种各样的理由，为什么我们活着？罗素在其自传的序言回答[我为何而活](http://www.awflasher.com/blog/archives/52)：  
>the longing for love,追求爱情  
>the search for knowledge，追求知识  
>unbearable  pity for the suffering of mankind.同情心

保尔·柯察金（Pavel Korchagin）说：  
>当他回忆往事的时候，他不会因为虚度年华而悔恨；也不会因为碌碌无为而羞愧  

让别人告诉你你自己活着为了什么，这听起来像是一个老师在对你说你应该这么活，不应该如何如何，然后老师告诉你这是某个所谓的『先哲』用一生来证明的哲学。   

whatever,we live just because we are alive;     
we struggle to live  because life is so suck;  
we wanna better life because that's from our heart.  

我们活着，想要更好的活着。这是事实，我们grow up，经历许多，忘记许多，我们需要bring our passion back.


## 关于坚持
2012年的时候，我在网上看到冯华君的文章[『凭什么是我们』](http://huajun.w18.net/?p=388)。里面写到：   
>当我们要把FIT输入法作为我们的核心产品重点发展的时候，有朋友提醒：输入法的词库，算法，交互设计你们都不是最强的，你们凭什么？  
>我们既不聪明又不富裕，我们凭什么？就凭我们愿意，**愿意用一点一滴的行动**去做好那些我们在意的事情。  

今天在逛知乎的时候看到一句话：   
>**梦想这种事情，就跟酒一样，越陈越香，要经得起时间的折磨**。  

还有多少人记得自己上一次的激情澎湃的宣言 which just like yesterday joke?  

我已经记不得我层许过多少无用的宣言。最近的一次是，1个月前开始的『我要每天做20个俯卧撑』，**坚持**了两周后计划慢慢停止。记得上次运动计划是在2012年夏天，每天跑步1600米，坚持了1个多月后停止。  

话语的分量在于你坚持的时间。  

找回你的梦想，坚持你的梦想。你就是那个值得骄傲的成功者。  

WOW,WOW,WOW，that's wonderful.棒极了，the processing of dreaming is awesome!!今天，你真棒！  

坚持。

## Android APP简赏(1)   

### APP的几个关注点   
android官网给出了几个APP设计的[关键点](http://developer.android.com/design/get-started/creative-vision.html)：  

惊喜，拟物。智能，简洁，读图，流畅，一致。  

下面我要介绍的Android APP有：  
[quora](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.quora.android:  
> [quora](http://www.quora.com/) Quora is your best source for knowledge. Ask any question, get real answers from people with first hand experience, and blog about what you know.  

[Pinterest](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.pinterest):  
>[Pinterest](http://about.pinterest.com/) is a tool for collecting and organizing things you love.

[flipboard](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=flipboard.app):    
>[Flipboard](http://flipboard.com/) is your personal magazine, filled with the things you care about. Catch up on the news, discover amazing things from around the world, or stay connected to the people closest to you--all in one place.

### pinterest 和 quora  

1. 真正的Android style。具体可以看谷歌官方的设计指南[Pure Android](http://developer.android.com/design/patterns/pure-android.html)    
2. 赏心悦目(Enchant me)，简单明了(Simplify my life))，令我痴狂(Make me amazing)。[Android vision](http://developer.android.com/design/get-started/creative-vision.html)    
3. 兼容性。在[设备显示](http://developer.android.com/design/style/devices-displays.html)上，两者不管是在横屏、竖屏还是(...平板手机？我只测试了平板，么有手机[悲伤])都能有很好的体验。  

### flipboard  
flipboard的APP截图我就不截图了。截图可以在google play上[查看](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=flipboard.app)。    
flipboard最初的[idea](http://www.ifanr.com/14816)就是社会化杂志，它把你的社交网络信息变成一本杂志。添加你的社交帐号之后，用美观的布局，大标题，大图片来优化阅读体验。  
当你翻开你的移动杂志，one world in your eyes,加上flipboard优秀的交互体验，更让阅读变得不可意思。或许，你只需要一个flipboard即可满足你的所有阅读体验。
